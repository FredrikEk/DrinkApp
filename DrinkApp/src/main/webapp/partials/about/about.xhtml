About

Using DrinkApp.nu without login in

You can use the Find Drink functionality freely without registering an account or login in, being able to browse for drinks entering a drink name and/or possible drink ingredients you might have at hand.

Using DrinkApp.nu as an authenticated user

As a registered and authenticated user you will be able to access alot of extensive functionality other than browsing for drink. You will for instance be able to add/edit/delete your own drinks to/in/from the database. You will have your own profile page, a page to hold your added drinks as well as a page for all your favourite drinks from the entire database. Registering is completely free of charge. Please note that the application currently is under construction and all functionality might not work a hundred percent as intended.
